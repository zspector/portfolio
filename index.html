About Me

My name is Zach and I am a JavaScript developer. My web development journey started a few years ago when I decided to take a Computer Science course on Coursera.org and became enthralled with code. I continued to play around JavaScript, HTML and CSS in my free time until I made the decision to go all in and enroll at MakerSquare, an immersive 12-week computer programming school in Austin, Texas. I am now excited to begin my journey as a JavaScript developer.

I am also an avid world traveler. I lived in Southeast Asia for three years and traveled extensively throughout the region. I have also explored Central America, Western Europe and parts of the Middle East.
